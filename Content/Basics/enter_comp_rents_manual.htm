Entering Competitor Rents

You should enter your comp rents each week. These instructions are for entering comp rents for competitors who don't use Automated Comps. For competitors who use Automated Comps, see reviewing and overriding competitor rents from Automated Comps.

To enter the rent for a competitor who doesn't use Automated Comps:

  1. Go to LRO>Competitors>Comp Rents.

  2. On the Comp Rents screen, select the competitor you want to update on the left.

  3. Click on the Effective Rent for the unit type, you want to update.

  4. Enter the Base Rent and Concession, if applicable.

    The Base Rent is the starting rent for a 12 month lease for that floorplan. The Concession is the total dollar value of the concession for a 12 month lease.

  5. Click the Green checkmark to save the rent.

  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each unit type you want to update.

  7. If desired, you can also enter the visits, leases, occupancy, and shop method and update the contact information at the top of the screen.

    Simply, click on the information you want to update and change it. When you click out of the field, the information will be saved.
