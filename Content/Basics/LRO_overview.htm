LRO Overview

The system is a powerful revenue management tool that will help ensure you are charging the right prices and offering the right lease terms to maximize your revenue.

and Your Property Management System

The system utilizes information from your PMS system to generate recommended lease terms and pricing. This means that it is important that certain key information is regularly entered into your PMS system.

Each night, the system extracts the data from your PMS system and uses it to generate pricing recommendations for the community. If the relationships between the system and your PMS system is bidirectional, the system will extract the information it needs from the PMS system and then push the pricing recommendations back to the PMS system. This provides a convenient way for your on-site teams to get the pricing each day.

Key Screens

There are some key screens you'll use in the system:

New Pricing : This is where you'll find the recommended pricing for each available unit at the community.

Renewal Pricing : This is where you'll find the renewal pricing for leases expiring in a particular month or date range. You can generate renewal offer letters from this screen.

Comp Rents : You'll enter information about your competitors' pricing here each week.

Pricing Trend : This is where you can get a snapshot of supply, demand, and pricing at the unit type level.

Lease Audit : Use this to find out if your on-site teams are following the pricing recommendations when leasing apartments.

Pricing Calls

An important part of using the system effectively is the weekly pricing call. The participants in these calls are typically the VP/Regional Manager, Community Manager, Leasing Team, Marketing, and Training. On each call, about 5 minutes is spent per community discussing pricing and goals for the community. Also, any concerns are discussed and any unusual circumstances that should be taken into account such as road construction projects that are going on near the community entrance.

Pricing calls are initially facilitated by a Rainmaker Business Consultant. After a contractual period of time, these are taken over in-house or a Rainmaker employee will continue to lead the pricing call if you have paid for Pricing Services.
