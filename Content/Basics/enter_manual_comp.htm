Entering a Manual Competitor

In the system, it's easy to add a competitor by selecting it on the map. However, there may be times when you can't find the competitor you are looking for on the map and need to enter it manually.

To enter a manual competitor:

  1. Go to LRO>Competitors>Comp Setup.
  2. On the Comp Setup screen, click Add Competitor.

  3. At the bottom left side of the screen, click Add Competitor Manually.

  4. Enter the name and address information for the competitor.

  5. Enter any additional information about the property such as contact information and website.
  6. Click Save.
  7. On the Comp Setup screen, click the pencil icon on the row for the competitor you just added.
  8. Verify the competitor information is correct. If not, click the Edit button for that section and change it. You can also enter information about why this community is a competitor and the competitor's utilities, amenities, and pet policies.
  9. Verify the unit type information and unit type mappings are correct.
  10. Click Close.
