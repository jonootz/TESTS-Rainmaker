Pricing Calls

Pricing calls are conducted each week. Initially, these will be run by a Business Consultant. After a contractual period of time, you'll run your pricing calls in house. If you want a Business Consultant to continue to run your pricing calls, you can sign up for Pricing Services.

What is the purpose of pricing calls?

The purpose of the pricing call is to look at pricing and leasing activity at each community. It is a time to have open, honest discussions about pricing with the site teams and others such as Marketing, Training, and Operations.

Who should be on the pricing call?

The people who are typically on the pricing call are:

VP/Regional Manager

Community Manager

Leasing Team

Marketing

Training

The pricing call will be facilitated by either a Business Consultant or your own in-house pricing manager/analyst.

What should the pricing call cover?

During the pricing call for each community, you should:

Review the Pricing Trend Report

What does your availability look like?

How do your rents compare with your competitors' rents?

Are your traffic and leasing numbers good?

How is your renewal retention?

Review New Pricing

Are you comfortable with pricing for the week?

Do any amenities need to be adjusted?

Provide Operational Insight

Why didn't prospects lease?

Are there specials in the market?

Did you have any non-compliant leases? If so, why?

Express any Pricing Concerns

You can also view the Pricing Call Dashboard under the Reporting tab to get a quick view of important information for your pricing call.
