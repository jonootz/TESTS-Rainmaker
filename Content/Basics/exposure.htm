Exposure

Exposure is the available vacant and on-notice apartments that are available to rent to prospective residents.

If an apartment is on-notice, this means that the resident who is currently living there has notified the office that they will be moving out and the apartment has not been rented to a new resident yet.

How is exposure used by the system?

Exposure is used by the DRRC in the calculation of reference rent.

You can view exposure graphs on the Pricing Trend report and on the Portfolio Trends Dashboard.
