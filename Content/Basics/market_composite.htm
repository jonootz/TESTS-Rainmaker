Market Composite

Market Composite is the average of the competitor rents with weights and positions applied.

Competitor rents should be entered once a week under LRO>Competitors>Comp Rents.

What are weights and positions?

Weights allow you to give more relevance to bigger competitors.

Positions allow your community to compare with competitors that have a better product or an inferior product.

Where are competitors set up?

Competitors are set up under LRO>Competitors>Comp Setup.

Competitor weights are set up under LRO>Competitors>Comp Weights.

Competitor positions are set up under LRO>Competitors>Comp Positions.

At the bottom of the Competitor Position screen, you can see Competitor Summary information including weights, positions, effective rent, and the market composite for each unit type.

How is the Market Composite used?

You can use the Market Composite to compare your competitor rent trend to your reference rent in the Rent Comparison section of the Pricing Trend report.
