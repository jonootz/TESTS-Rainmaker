Pricing Overview

This is a very simplified overview of how the LRO system generates prices.

  1. The DRRC considers Exposure and Leasing Velocity to calculate the Reference Rent.
  2. The Reference Rent is then fed into the Optimizer.
  3. The Supply Forecast (what is available to rent and what is expected to come available) and Demand Forecast (how many leases/move-ins do we expect to get) are also fed into the Optimizer.
  4. The Optimizer generates the Daily Pricing Recommendations which appear on the pricing reports.
