The system utilizes information from your PMS system to generate recommended lease terms and pricing. This means that it is important that certain key information is regularly entered into your PMS system.

The following information that you'll enter into your PMS system is important for the system to have to generate optimized pricing. It is important that this information is entered as quickly as possible, most likely daily.

Guest Cards: These capture the visits of prospective residents (traffic). The system uses the date of the visit, the desired floorplan/unit type, and the desired move-in date.

Initial Lease: The system uses the move-in date and the lease start/end date.

Notice to Vacate: The system uses the date of notice and the move-out date.

Make Ready Dates: This is when an apartment will be move-in ready. The system uses this date.

Renewal Lease: The system uses the date the renewal was signed and the renewal lease start/end date.

The system uses the guest card information to see:

  • when prospects are looking to move in (Demand)
  • what unit types the prospects are interested in
  • how far in advance of the desired move-in date a prospect typically looks for an apartment (Lead Time)

The system uses the various dates to see:

  • if a resident is moving out (Supply)
  • when leases expire (Potential Future Supply)
  • how many residents typically renew
  • when an apartment will be ready for a new resident to move in
