Availability and Demand Forecasting

The system uses advanced statistical methods to forecast availability and demand for 16 weeks into the future to provide pricing for new leases and renewals.

The number of weeks into the future is set by parameter 1 - Opt Horizon which is set at 16 by default.

The system considers historical and current information from the PMS system when making pricing recommendations.

The forecast of Availability includes:

Vacant Units: Units that are vacant and available to be rented.

On-notice Units: Units are currently occupied, but the current residents have given notice to move out and the units have not been rented to new residents yet.

Estimated renewals and early terminations: Trends in lease renewals and early terminations as determined by past customer behavior.

The Move-in Demand forecast considers the following based on historical information from the PMS system:

Lead Time Curve: How far ahead of time do your prospects shop for apartments

Seasonality Curve: Are there certain times of the year when demand increases or decreases

Renewal Retention: How likely are your residents to renew their leases

Price-Demand Relationship: At what price are prospects/residents motivated to lease or renew
