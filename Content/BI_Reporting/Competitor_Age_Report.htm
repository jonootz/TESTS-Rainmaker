Competitor Age Report (BI Reporting)

The Competitor Age Report can be accessed from the Reporting tab in the Web UI.

This report gives you two key pieces of information:

  • whether the rents are being updated weekly (Status column)
  • whether the rents are actually changing from week to week (Date Rent Changed and Rent Change Aging columns)

This information is useful to determine if your communities are shopping their comps or if there is an issue with a competitor. If there is an issue with a competitor, you may need to change its weight/position or even remove it as a competitor.

Viewing the Report

The report will open when you click the Competitor Age link on the Reporting tab.

In addition to the Competitor Age information, there is a Definitions page. You can switch between them using the tabs at the bottom of the screen.

Comps that are not weighted are excluded from the Competitor Age Report.

Learn about filtering the Competitor Age Report

The information on the report is divided into sections by community.

Competitor Name (Code) comes from the Comp Setup screen.

Competitor Unit Type comes from the Comp Setup screen.

Date Rent Changed is the last time the rents were changed on the Comp Rents screen.

Rent Change Aging is the number of days since the rents were changed on the Comp Rents screen.

Last Update Aging is the number of days since the rents were saved or the Stale banner was dismissed on the Comp Rents screen. The rents may or may not have actually changed.

Last Update User allows you to see who the last person was to save the rents or dismiss the Stale banner on the Comp Rents screen. They may or may not have actually changed the rents.

Status lets you know if a competitor is stale or not. A competitor is stale if the rents haven't been saved in the past week. (The default setting for stale competitors is 7 days. This is set by parameter 105.) If this column is blank, then the competitor is not stale.

A competitor can be "not stale" even if the Rent Change Aging is greater than 7 (parameter 105). This can happen if a user is just dismissing the Stale banner and not changing the rent.

Definitions

The last tab is a definitions page that will help you understand the information contained in the report.
