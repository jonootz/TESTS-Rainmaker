RPU Report

  • How can I prove to my owners that the system is working?
  • How much has my revenue grown?
  • Are we giving up occupancy and increasing rents more or are we giving up too much occupancy and not recovering on rents?
  • Given our current vacancy, are we making it up in revenue?
  • My owners think we should be at 99% occupancy, but I disagree. How can I prove that we can make more money with a lower occupancy and pushing rents?

Learn more about the RPU Report

Summary Analysis Report

  • How have we performed since go live?
  • What has my rent growth been over the last year/quarter?
  • How am I doing compared to last year?
  • What was my asking price, occupancy, exposure, etc. last year?
  • What is my new lease or renewal lease rent change?

Learn more about the Summary Analysis Report

Renewals Report

  • How do my City X communities compare to my City Y communities in terms of rent, occupancy, exposure, etc.?
  • Are my communities with Fee Manager X doing better than those with Fee Manager Y?
  • Is my renewal strategy too aggressive, too conservative, or just right?
  • How many renewals do I have expiring and how many have been committed?
  • How have we been performing on renewals since go live?

Learn more about the Renewals Report

Old to New Differential Report

  • Are my replacement rents higher?
  • Am I getting more money over each prior lease?
  • What kind of revenue lift am I getting over the prior lease, new and renewal?
  • Are my new leases higher since go live?

Learn more about the Old to New Differential Report
