Daily Pulse 60 Day New Lease Details Report

The Daily Pulse 60 Day New Lease Details report is opened from the Detail View of the Daily Pulse report. It gives you a way to get the details behind the 60 Day Average Effective Rent amounts on the Daily Pulse report.

Opening the Report

To open the report, on the Detail View of the Daily Pulse report, click any of the numbers for the 60 Day Average Effective rent for the desired community or community and unit type to view the lease details that make up those numbers.

Tips

Prompts

The Daily Pulse 60 Plus New Lease Details report shows information for the community or community and unit type for the average effective rent you selected. You can click the Refresh button and then use the Prompts dialog to view the New Lease Details for different hierarchies (reporting groups), communities, contract dates, and unit types.

Definitions

There is a Definitions tab to help you understand the information on the report.
