Getting Started with the Mobile App

The Mobile App contains the key features of that are most commonly used by the leasing site teams and pricing and revenue managers.

How do I get it?

Mobile is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can find it in the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store. Simply search for LRO.

Once you have installed the app, launch it, then enter the same login credentials you use for the LRO web application. You will also need to enter your client name. Your client name can be found in your LRO web URL where it says "lrodemo" in the sample below.

If you encounter any issues, please email LROsupport@letitrain.com.

Supported OS's and Devices

Mobile works with a variety of Apple and Android devices.
