Parameters at Work: Increase Rate

Increase Rate

The Increase Rate is set on the Renewal Config Parameters screen which you can find at Admin>Parameters>Renewal Config.

This parameter is entered in the Rate field for the range or ranges that are greater than 0.

Purpose

This parameter allows you to specify how much of the difference between the resident's current rent and the new move-in rent should the rent be increased for the renewal offer. This comes into play when the new move-in rent is greater than the resident's current rent.

Example

Increase Rate: 70%

Resident's Current Rent: $1500

New Move-In Rent $1800

Difference between Resident's Current Rent and the New Move-In Rent: $300 ($1800 - $1500)

Renewal Increase: $210 ($300 X 70%)

Renewal Offer: $1710 ($1500 + $210)

The renewal increase may be capped by the Max Increase parameter or may have a minimum increase required by the Min Increase parameter.
