Parameters Recipe Cards

Parameters allow you to communicate your business goals to the LRO system so that it considers these when calculating prices. There are many parameters and knowing which ones to change, when to change them, and what to change them to can be overwhelming.

To help you, we've come up with some suggestions. The following parameters pertain to pricing for New Leases and for Renewals. We've suggested values that you can set each parameter during Average, Soft, and Peak demand seasons. These parameter settings can be changed as needed to meet the business objectives of each community.

The Drop Rate, Increase Rate, Minimum Increase, and Maximum Increase are specified on the Renewal Config Parameters screen.

To find these parameters, go to Admin>Parameters>Renewal Config.

On this screen, you'll enter the rates and increase limits for different ranges based on the percentage difference from what the resident is currently paying and the new lease pricing for that unit. The suggested settings are based on the default ranges that are explained below.

  • From 0.00 To >0.00 - The current perceived rent is less than the move-in rate
  • From 0.00 To 0.00 - The current perceived rent is equal to the move-in rate
  • From 0.00 To <0.00 - The current perceived rent is more than the move-in rate
