Days on Market Analysis Parameters

Turning on Days on Market Analysis

To use Days on Market Analysis, the following parameters must be set to 1:

Parameter 276, Perform Days on Market Analysis

Parameter 266, Days on Market Statistics

Days on Market Analysis will be available after you turn it on for the community and the nightly processes have run.
