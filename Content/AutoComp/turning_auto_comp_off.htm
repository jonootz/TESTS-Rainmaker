Turning Automated Comps Off for a Competitor or Unit Type

Automated Comps can be turned on and off at the Community, Competitor, and Unit Type level. Automated Comps is turned off at the community level using parameter 317.

Turning Automated Comps Off for a Competitor

If Automated Comps is not returning acceptable rents and you are having to override them most of the time for a particular competitor, you may want to turn it off for that competitor. To do this:

  1. On the Comp Setup screen, click the pencil icon in the Actions column for the competitor.
  2. Click Edit on the Competitor Information section.

  3. Change the Automated Comp Settings toggle to Off.

  4. Click Save.

    Automated Comps will no longer enter comp rents for this competitor. You will need to shop and manually enter the comp rent.

Turning Automated Comps Off for a Competitor Unit Type

If Automated Comps is not returning acceptable rents and you are having to override them most of the time for a particular competitor unit type, you may want to turn it off for that competitor unit type. To do this:

  1. On the Comp Setup screen, click the pencil icon in the Actions column for the competitor.
  2. In the Unit Types section, click on the unit type you want to edit.

  3. Change the Automated Comp Settings toggle to Off.

  4. Click Save.

    Automated Comps will no longer enter comp rents for this competitor unit type. You will need to shop and manually enter the comp rent.
