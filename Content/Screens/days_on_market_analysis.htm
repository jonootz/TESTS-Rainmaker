Days on Market Analysis

Days on Market Analysis would typically be used by Pricing Analysts.

To use Days on Market Analysis, parameter 276 must be set to 1 for the community, then the nightly processes must be run.

Viewing Days on Market Analysis

To view Days on Market Analysis, go to LRO>Rev Mgmt>Days on Market Analysis.

By default, the screen will show units that are available for rent. Typically, this would be units with the following statuses: On Notice, Vacant Ready, and Vacant Unavailable. However, you can show all units, if you want to.

Leasing pace zones are Slow, Medium, and Fast. The leasing pace is determined by the unit's days on market as compared to the average for the LRO unit type.

You can click the Historical Leasing Pace for a unit to see which leases were used in calculating the unit's historical leasing pace. Parameters 264 and 265 are used to determine the time frame for the leases used in the calculation.

If there isn't enough data to calculate the historical leasing pace for a unit, then the historical leasing pace will just be set to Medium.

Tips

You can mouse over a column heading to get a tip about the information displayed in that column.

You can print the Days on Market Analysis or send it to Excel, if you want to.

Filtering Days on Market Analysis

You can use the filters to specify the information that appears on the screen.

  • You can choose to show all units instead of just the ones that are available to rent.
  • You can filter the information by unit type.
  • You can search for a particular unit by unit ID.

The Refresh button will set all of these filters back to their default values.

You can click on a column heading to sort the information by that column.
