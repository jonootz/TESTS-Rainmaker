Enabling Editing

You must click Enable Editing to make any changes on the Unit Categories, Unit Types, and PMS Unit Types screens (under Configuration in the Web UI). When you enable editing, other users are locked out of the community and the editing options appear on the screen. After you have saved your changes, you will need to click Unlock Property to allow other users back in.

To enable editing:

  1. Click Enable Editing. (If editing is already enabled, you'll see an Unlock Property button instead.)

  2. You'll receive a message asking if you're sure you want to enable editing since this will lock out other users. Click OK.

You may want to let other users know when you are going to do this and/or plan to do it at a time when other users aren't typically in the system.

After you have saved all of your changes, click Unlock Property to allow other users back into the community.
